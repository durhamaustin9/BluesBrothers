Meet The Band

Jim

Jim Elam starting playing guitar at the age of 15. He studied classical guitar in North Carolina and at Arizona State University where he received his Master's degree in music. After graduating, Jim switched to electric guitar and played for 12 years with the Blues/Soul band "The Lynwood Flyers". During the daylight hours, Jim enjoys teaching computer classes at Scottsdale Community College.

Doug

Doug Sawyer started playing the bass professionally at age 14 when he joined the New Relations with current Branston star Doug Gabriel. He later played with the Silver Dollar Band with current international blues guitarist Craig Erickson. In 1992, he joined the Phoenix Blues group, the Lynwood Flyers. Doug played locally and on the road in NM, LA, and TX with Aaron Thompson in 2005-2008.

After graduating, Paul switched to harmonica and played for 12 years with the Blues/Soul band "The Lynwood Flyers". Paul enjoys teaching computer classes at Scottsdale Community College.
